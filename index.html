Acute Stroke Protocol Guidance

    • CTAS 1 and/or uncorrected ABC problem
    • Symptoms resolved prior to paramedic arrival
    • Blood sugar < 3 mmol/L (unless symptoms persist after correction)
    • Seizure at onset of symptoms
    • Glasgow Coma Scale < 10
    • Terminally ill or palliative care patient
    • Transport out of hospital will exceed 2 hours

    More on stroke protocol: For DSC indications, LVO/CTAS reporting, and contraindications from BLS PCS, open the Acute Stroke Protocol - Prompt Card under Destination Guidelines.
